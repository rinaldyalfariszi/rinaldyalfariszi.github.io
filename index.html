Who am I?

My name is Muhammad Rinaldy Alfariszi, but you can call me Rey. I am an undergraduate student at Telkom University and a Unity Game Developer.

Why do I find game development interesting?

Well, I can say that it's my childhood dream job. However, back then, I didn't have any knowledge about programming or math at all. When I went to college, I acquired the skills necessary to become a good game developer. I mean, everyone has to start from somewhere, right?

So.. what can I do?

I have experience in developing multiple game projects for the Windows and Android mobile platforms using C# Programming Language. I have worked with various interactive technologies, including Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Hand Tracking, and Eye Tracking.
