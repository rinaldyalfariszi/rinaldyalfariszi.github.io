Project VR Oculus Quest 2 Game Harfish VR

A Virtual Reality (VR) game where the goal is to breed the fish by following the given steps from start to finish.

Status : Discontinued

Platform : Android (Oculus Quest 2)

Technology : Virtual Reality, Unity Game Engine, Blender 3D

Demo video of Game Harfish VR

My team and I were assigned to develop a Virtual-Reality (VR) version of the Harfish game called Harfish VR. Thanks to my team's outstanding performance, we received funding from the company and were invited to work at one of our company's offices in Jakarta. It was an opportunity for us to learn and develop the game using Oculus Quest 2.

Unfortunately, things didn't go as planned, and the game had to be discontinued because the project took a different direction after initially being developed for the fisheries industry as a VR game.

From this project, I learned about the dynamic work culture in the company's office, the technical aspects of developing a game for the Oculus Quest 2 device, and the importance of making critical decisions as a team.
