Project Mobile AR Game Budi Daya Visual Novel with AR

An Interactive AR educational mobile game with the genre of Visual Novel where players need to build and take care of their fish farm by experiencing it from the main character perspective.

Status : Published (itch.io)

Platform : Android

Technology : Augmented Reality, Unity Game Engine, Blender 3D

Pitching video of Budi Daya Visual Novel with AR

This is my first paid game project where I worked with a team of 9 people that later grew to 24 people. The game utilizes a unique concept by combining Augmented Reality (AR) with a Visual Novel experience.

Core Mechanics

The gameplay of Budi Daya Visual Novel with AR

This game features a simple mechanic where player have to follow the dialogue, similar to a visual novel. However, the unique aspect is that the game utilizes AR technology, allowing player to build and take care of their fish farm from the perspective of the main character.

What have I worked on?

  • Developed a Visual Novel tool with ScriptableObjects, to create and store the character names, dialogues, and animations. The tool could also change the background environment.

  • Developed a basic economic system where players could buy things using in-game virtual currency using C# scripting.

  • Utilized Vuforia SDK to implement Augmented Reality (AR) content
